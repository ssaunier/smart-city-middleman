Ticket for Le Wagon - Passport for the future

A 9-week coding bootcamp
offered to 5 young adults of Brussels

You're between 18 and 35 years old?
You're passionate about the world of web & new technologies?
You want to learn to code and create web apps from A to Z?
Maybe you even dream of making it your job? Or work in a startup?

This fall, Maks vzw and Le Wagon Brussels will offer 5 young adults of Brussels, unemployed and/or with limited financial resources, the possibility to join 20 young entrepreneurs from all over the world to take part in the next Le Wagon Brussels Coding Bootcamp. This 9-week coding bootcamp is an intensive full-time training in web application development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Ruby, Rails,...), running from October 17 until December 16, 2016.

The outcome? The ability to develop your own applications for any project you want to build, an immersion in a stimulating international & entrepreneurial environment (at Co.Station, in the center of Brussels), the acquisition of tech competences in high demand on the job market and, finally, a 3-month internship (at least!) in a startup from the Brussels Region.

If you want to get some help to complete the application form, don't hesitate to contact Jan (jan@maksvzw.org), from Maks vzw. Jan & his team will accompany you throughout the selection process to put all the chances on your side!
